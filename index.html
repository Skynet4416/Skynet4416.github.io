About Us

Skynet #4416 is a FIRST Robotics Competition team based in Ramat HaSharon, Israel. The team includes over 20 students from the local high schools “Alon” and “Rothberg”, representing the city. The team was founded in 2011 under the name “Team Rocket”, based in “Alon” High School, and expanded after two years of activity to include the whole city. After the 2016-2017 season, the team rebranded, into “Skynet”.
Our mission statement is: to serve as a beacon of leadership and social and personal entrepreneurship within the FIRST community and outside it. Our main goal is, to become a STEAM center that allows the sharing of knowledge and connection between communities, while maintaining a tolerant and equitable environment to all. Our leading values in achieving these goals are creativity, entrepreneurship, learning – both personal and social – and tolerance. ----------------------------------------------------------------- Contact us!

The 1/10 Project focuses on one question – What percentage of teenagers smoke? According to polls we conducted, most people believe around 60%. The estimate, made by central establishments in smoking prevention, is actually only 10%. After compiling the poll results, we delved into the question – Why? What is the reason that most of us believe the percentage of teenage smokers is that much higher than it is? The reason we came to is that smokers have an active sign: The cigarette, the smell, and even the media coverage smokers get.
